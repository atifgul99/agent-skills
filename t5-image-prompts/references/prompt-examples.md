## Example 1: Mario Testino — "Wet Silk" (High-Key)

**Signal:** High-Gloss Eroticism / Sheer Tension
**Model:** Imagen 4
**Aspect Ratio:** 3:4

```
[AR 3:4] Medium-Shot High-Gloss Editorial for Vogue Paris by Mario Testino. Shot on Contax 645, 80mm Planar lens. 5600K Hard Frontal Flash, +1.0 EV High Key. Composition: Subject anchors center, clinical white wall throws sharp shadow halo behind.

SUBJECT: 29yo Swedish supermodel, facial blend of Elsa Hosk and Elin Nordegren. Facial Architecture: sharp mandibular angle, high-set malar definition, wide-set blue eyes. Skin Tone: Deep-bronzed with high-specular sweat sheen. Expression: Candid, high-energy moment of spontaneous euphoria.

LIGHTING: Signature Testino frontal light — hard-edge specular highlights with circular catchlights. The light saturates skin tone and eliminates digital gray mid-tones. Compressed highlights preserve texture detail.

STYLING: She wears a single layer of drenched, ultra-sheer white silk chiffon. The fabric is wet and translucent, suffering capillary action, adhering strictly to the sweat-dampened skin.

DETAILS: Visible epidermal pores on cheeks. Fine vellus hair catching rim light. Perspiration sheen. Thermodynamics: Evaporative cooling triggers goosebumps beneath the wet silk. Vibrant Saturated Color Grading.
```

**Audit Notes:**

- Hero Element: Wet silk chiffon (SCAFFOLD: adhering to body, SURFACE: sheer/translucent, LIGHT: transmissive)
- Dermal: ✓ pores, ✓ vellus hair, ✓ evaporative cooling response
- The Sting: Goosebumps beneath wet silk (physics: evaporative cooling)
- Token estimate: ~190

**Finishing Concept:** Scalability Ladder

```

Centered symbol: an upward-pointing chevron constructed from two overlapping parallelograms — left parallelogram in warm gold (#D4A843), right parallelogram in bright mint (#34D399). **Audit Notes:**

- Scalability Ladder: ✓ 16px (green square + bright chevron shape visible), 32px (color overlap readable), 512px (full geometric precision)
