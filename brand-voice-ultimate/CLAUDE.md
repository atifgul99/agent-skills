# Brand Voice Ultimate

Complete brand voice system — analyze, extract, build, and enforce brand voice across all channels. Combines URL-based brand analysis, personal voice extraction, build-from-scratch questionnaire, competitor comparison, consistency auditing, messaging hierarchy, and AI content enforcement.

## Modes

- **analyze** — Full brand analysis from URL (dimensions, archetype, competitor comparison, consistency audit)
- **extract** — Voice profile from writing samples (phrases, confidence zones, rhythm, anti-patterns)
- **build** — Voice profile from scratch via 15-question framework (identity, audience, positioning, aspiration)
- **full** — All three + messaging hierarchy + enforcement
